Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

SecureInk offers a customer-friendly refund policy for digital security subscriptions purchased directly from us.

1. Money-Back Guarantee

  • Annual Subscriptions: Full refund if requested within 30 days of initial purchase or renewal date.
  • Monthly Subscriptions: Full refund if requested within 14 days of purchase. Subsequent months are non-refundable.

2. How to Request a Refund

Contact our support team at support@secureink.in or through your account dashboard. Provide order details and reason (optional). Refunds processed within 7–10 business days to original payment method.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

  • After the guarantee period expires.
  • Subscriptions purchased through third-party resellers (check their policy).
  • Renewals where no request was made within the window.
  • Abuse of refund policy (e.g., repeated purchases/refunds).

4. Cancellations

You may cancel auto-renewal anytime via account settings. Access continues until subscription end date (no partial refunds for remaining period).

We aim for 100% satisfaction with our protection services. Thank you for choosing SecureInk.
