Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

1. Introduction

SecureInk India ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our website, antivirus software, identity protection services, and related products ("Services").

We act as a data controller and comply with applicable laws including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act) and global standards like GDPR where relevant.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing information (when you purchase or register).
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, device type, OS version, browser type, unique device identifiers, scan results, threat logs (for antivirus functionality).
  • Security & Threat Data: Malware samples, suspicious URLs, file hashes, browsing behavior for threat detection (anonymized where possible).
  • Identity Monitoring Data: If subscribed — credit-related alerts, dark web mentions (processed securely, not stored unnecessarily).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our antivirus, VPN, identity protection, and other Services.
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to malware, ransomware, phishing, and cyber threats.
  • Process payments, manage subscriptions, and send service updates/notifications.
  • Conduct anonymized threat research and improve detection engines.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights/users.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We share data only when necessary:

  • With service providers (cloud hosting, payment processors, analytics — under strict contracts).
  • For legal compliance, fraud prevention, or safety (e.g., court orders).
  • In case of merger/acquisition (with notice where required).

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS), access controls, regular audits, and threat monitoring to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure — we continuously enhance protections aligned with our mission as a cybersecurity company.

6. Your Rights

You may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent (where processing is consent-based).
  • Opt-out of certain processing (e.g., marketing emails).

Contact: privacy@secureink.in

7. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with the updated date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
